Accessibility Statement

We want every homeowner to be able to use this website, including people who use assistive technology. Here is our commitment and how to reach us if something is hard to use.

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Our commitment

White Leaf Roofing is committed to making its website usable by as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, which is the widely accepted standard for accessible websites, and we treat accessibility as an ongoing effort rather than a one-time task.

What we have done

This website was built with accessibility in mind, including:

  • Clear, semantic page structure with proper headings and labeled regions.
  • Text alternatives (alt text) for meaningful images.
  • Descriptive labels on every form field, and helpful error messages.
  • A "skip to content" link and full keyboard navigation, with a visible focus outline.
  • Strong color contrast between text and backgrounds.
  • A responsive layout that works on phones, tablets, and computers, and respects your system's reduced-motion setting.
  • Descriptive link text (no vague "click here" links).

Ways to make browsing easier

  • Zoom the page: use your browser's zoom (Ctrl or Cmd and the + key) or your device's text-size settings to enlarge text.
  • Keyboard navigation: use the Tab key to move between links and buttons, and Enter to activate them.
  • Screen readers and voice control: the site is built to work with common assistive tools such as screen readers and speech-recognition software.

Known limitations

Despite our efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible, and third-party content we embed (such as videos) may follow its own accessibility standards. We are working to identify and fix any issues, and we welcome your feedback.

Need help, or found a problem?

If you have trouble using any part of this website, or you would like information in a different format, please contact us. We will help you directly and work to fix the issue:
