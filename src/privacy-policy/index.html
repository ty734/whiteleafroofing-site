Information we collect

Information you give us. When you fill out our callback or contact forms, we collect the details you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, and any message about your roof. When you call or text us, we receive your phone number and the content of your message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we and our analytics and advertising partners automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, including your IP address, device and browser type, the pages you view, how you arrived at our site, and similar usage data. This is collected through cookies and similar technologies (see below).

Phone call information. We use a call-tracking service to understand which of our marketing efforts lead to calls. When you call a number on this site, that service may record the call and collect the caller's number, call time, and duration. Calls may be recorded for quality and record-keeping.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry, schedule your free inspection, and provide an estimate.
  • To contact you about your request by phone, text, or email.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website.
  • To measure and improve our advertising, and understand which sources bring us customers.
  • To keep records and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use the details you submit to add you to marketing or mailing lists.

How we share your information

We share information only as needed to run our business:

  • Service providers that operate this site and our tools on our behalf, including our website host and lead-storage provider (Vercel and its storage partner), our email delivery provider (Resend), and our security/anti-spam provider (Cloudflare).
  • Analytics and advertising partners, primarily Google, through Google Analytics and Google Ads, to measure site usage and advertising performance.
  • When required by law, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Cookies, analytics & advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics (Google Analytics), and advertising measurement (Google Ads). Third parties, including Google, may use cookies to show you ads on other sites based on your visit here. You have choices:

  • You can set your browser to refuse or delete cookies. Some site features may not work as well.
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics with the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • You can manage personalized Google ads at Google My Ad Center, and general opt-outs at optout.aboutads.info.

Calls and text messages

By giving us your phone number through this site, by call, or by text, you agree that we may contact you by phone call and text message about your request and your project. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP or by telling us, and you can ask us to stop calling at any time.

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you have given us, or ask us to stop contacting you, by emailing andy@wlcbuilt.com or calling 480-363-2898.
