Can you repair my tile roof without replacing it?

Usually, yes. Cracked or slipped tiles can be reset or matched, and a failed section of underlayment can be re-felted on its own with your original tiles relaid over it. A full replacement only comes up when the repair math stops working, and you'll see the numbers first.
