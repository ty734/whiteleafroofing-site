The re-felt and relay story

Your tiles are fine. The felt under them isn't.

Here's the thing most roofing companies working Sun Lakes won't lead with: on a concrete tile roof, the tile is armor, not waterproofing. The underlayment beneath it is what actually keeps rain out of your house, and Arizona heat wears it out decades before the tiles quit. Tile roofs are "lifetime roofs" the way a car is a "lifetime car" if you replace the engine.

So most tile roof replacement in Sun Lakes isn't really new tile. It's a tile underlayment replacement: we lift your tiles, re-felt the deck, and relay the same tiles. Your roof looks exactly like it did, matches the street exactly like it did, and costs far less than a roof full of new tile you didn't need.

If someone bid you a complete tear-off with all new tiles, it's worth a free second opinion before you sign. Sometimes their bid is fair. I'll tell you either way.
