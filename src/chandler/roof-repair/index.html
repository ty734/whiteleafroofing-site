Chandler repair questions

What homeowners ask me

How much does roof repair cost in Chandler, AZ?

Most repairs run $800 to $4,000 depending on what's wrong and how much material is involved. Anything past about $4,000 usually means a replacement makes more sense, and I'll show you exactly why before you spend a dime. Every job gets a free inspection and an individual bid.

How fast can you get to a roof leak in Chandler?

We're based right here in Chandler at Gilbert Rd and Queen Creek Rd, so I can usually get to an active leak the same or next day. Leaks and fresh storm damage go to the front of the line.

Can you fix a tile roof without replacing the whole thing?

Very often, yes. Slipped or cracked tiles can be reset or swapped, and a small section of failed underlayment can be repaired on its own. If the underlayment is failing across the whole roof, we lift your tiles, re-felt, and relay the same tiles instead of selling you a brand new roof.

Will you try to talk me into a full replacement?

No. Repairs come first at White Leaf. I only bring up replacement when the repair math stops making sense for you, and when that happens I'll walk you through the numbers so you can see it yourself.

Can a monsoon damage my roof without any visible leak?

Yes. Monsoon wind can lift tiles, shift flashing, and tear underlayment without a single drip showing up inside. That's why I do free walk-around inspections after big storms, so small damage gets caught before the next one turns it into water in your ceiling.
