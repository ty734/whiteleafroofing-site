Common questions

Asked and answered

How much does roofing cost in Chandler and Gilbert?

It depends entirely on what your roof actually needs, which is why every job is bid individually and the inspection is free. I look at the roof myself and give you a real number on the spot, with material and underlayment options at different price points. For honest ranges and the factors that move them, see the Arizona roof cost guide.

Is the roof inspection really free?

Yes. It costs nothing, I show up personally, walk the roof myself, and talk you through what I find. No salesmen, no obligation, no pressure.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential replacements are done in 2 to 4 days depending on the size and complexity of the roof.

Can you reuse my existing roof tiles?

In many cases, yes. If your tiles are in good shape we lift them, replace the failed underlayment beneath, and relay the same tiles. It saves real money and it's one of the most common jobs we do.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Licensed, bonded and insured, Arizona ROC #325377. You can verify it yourself on the Arizona Registrar of Contractors site, and I encourage you to.
