Is it time?

When a Gold Canyon roof is done

Homes in Gold Canyon and Peralta Trails sit in exposed open desert. No tree cover, no neighboring rooftops running interference, just full sun on the roof from the moment it clears the Superstitions. Tile handles that exposure well. The underlayment beneath it doesn't, and in full sun it cooks faster than the same felt would a few miles west.

That's why a Gold Canyon roof can look perfect from the driveway and still be failing. The tile is armor; the underlayment underneath is the actual waterproofing. Watch for slipped or cracked tiles after wind, stains on ceilings or walls, musty smells in the attic, and leaks that show up with the first monsoon of the season.

The free inspection settles it. I drive out, walk the roof myself, show you photos of what I find, and tell you honestly whether it's a repair, a replacement, or nothing at all yet.
